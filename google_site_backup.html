Lost SMSF Deed

T Legal explains which document to order by four simple steps below

Order Lost SMSF Deed Replacement

Price: $165

Fixed price incl GST

Four Simple Steps Below

SMSF - Lost Trust Deed

1Lost SMSF Deed

Scenario:

  • SMSF Trust Deed is lost

  • You have checked with the original document provider / law firm

  • You have previous years' SMSF tax returns

Explained:

The SMSF Deed Update is designed for these scenarios:

  • Lost SMSF trust deed
    (or lost recent SMSF deed update, where
    older SMSF trust deed version is still available)

  • Invalid SMSF trust deed
    (e.g. missing consents / signatures, deceased party)

  • Deceased member
    (i.e. requiring new SMSF trust deed
    showing current parties)

T Legal provides expert review of the lost current SMSF trust deed situation as part of preparation of your replacement SMSF trust deed

2 Order a SMSF Deed Update

Solution:

  • SMSF Trust Deed is easily replaced for fixed fee

  • No new SMSF setup or lodgment is needed

  • A SMSF Deed Update will replace the old SMSF Trust Deed

Explained:

You can easily replace your SMSF trust deed:

  • It's a rollover, and there is no trust resettlement for updating or replacing a lost SMSF trust deed with a new version where the assets and members of the SMSF do not change.

  • SMSF trust deeds are therefore updated (called a "deed update") every 5-7 years to ensure the SMSF is administered according to the latest changes in superannuation law and legal features.

  • This not available for a discretionary trust or a unit trust.

Contact T Legal on the number / email below or submit a response to the SMSF deed update / lost deed online order form

3Sign the SMSF Deed Update and Update SMSF Records

Signing:

  • Follow signing instructions included in SMSF Deed Update

  • The SMSF Deed Update must be signed and dated

  • The SMSF Accountant must be given a copy

Explained:

Ensure all SMSF Deed Update document package contents are signed:

  • The SMSF Deed Update document package contains all you need to replace the lost SMSF trust deed

  • But it is not in effect until it is fully signed and dated

Is other SMSF documentation also lost? Do you need to:

  • Order a SMSF corporate trustee Constitution?
    (see T Legal Constitution Adoption / Upgrade Order Form)

  • Re-sign ATO SMSF Trustee Declarations for each Member?
    (see ATO SMSF Trustee Declaration Form)

T Legal provides expert review of the lost current SMSF trust deed situation to confirm if other lost items also need to be replaced and to check your documentation is correctly signed

4 Important - Check BDBNs and Reversionary Pensions

Important:

  • Check if any BDBNs or Reversionary Pensions were made earlier

  • BDBNs and Reversionary Pensions should be replaced too

  • Don't rely on BDBNs and Reversionary Pensions made under the lost SMSF Deed

Explained:

A BDBN or reversionary pension is reliant on the SMSF trust deed:

  • Which contains signing requirements applicable at the time the earlier BDBN or Reversionary Pension Nomination was signed

  • If the SMSF trust deed applicable at that earlier time is lost, you cannot confirm if the BDBN or Reversionary Pension Nomination remains effective.

T Legal SMSF deed update package includes a "low doc" BDBN that can be easily signed as a first step. You should consider ordering a traditional BDBN to replace the low doc BDBN.
